# Where to Find "Add Product" in Meta Developer Console

## Location

Based on your screenshot, you're in the right place!

### Step-by-Step:

1. **You're already here:** "My Apps" tab (top navigation)

2. **Look at the main content area:**
   - You should see your app "Stories" 
   - Look for a section that says "Products" or "Add Product"
   - OR look for a button/icon that says "Add platform" or "+ Add Product"

3. **Alternative locations:**
   - **Left sidebar:** Look for "Products" or "Add Product" link
   - **Dashboard:** Go to app dashboard, look for "Add Product" button
   - **Settings:** In "App settings", there might be a "Products" section

4. **If you see "Add platform" button:**
   - Click it
   - This might be the same as "Add Product"
   - Look for "Instagram Basic Display" or "Instagram Graph API" in the list

## If You Can't Find It

**Alternative:**
1. Go directly to: https://developers.facebook.com/apps/{your-app-id}/dashboard/
2. Replace `{your-app-id}` with your app ID
3. Look for "Add Product" in the dashboard

**Or:**
1. Click on your app name "Stories" in the sidebar
2. Go to Dashboard
3. Look for "Products" section
4. Click "Add Product"
